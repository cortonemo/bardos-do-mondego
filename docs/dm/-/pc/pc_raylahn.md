Raylahn appears as a young adult human with an athletic build, shaped by military training and mercenary life. He stands around 5’11”, and his rigid posture conveys discipline and authority. His straight black hair, shoulder-length, is usually tied back in a simple braid to stay out of the way in combat. His grayish-blue eyes, cold and intense, always seem to assess his surroundings.
