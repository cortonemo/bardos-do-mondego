                                                                                                                                   | **Group Identity / Cell Profile**<br><br>The **FunGuys** (also known as **Funguys**) are a group of **Player Characters (PCs)** who united early in the campaign. Their name derives directly from the nature of one of their first major missions involving an **alien fungal infestation** tied to the [Alien Parasitic Fungi](fungos_parasitas_alienigenas.md). The group was officially formed when the **[Tavernkeeper](Free City of Grehawk/npc_innkeeper.md)** assigned **[Vaunia Kolakiala](pc_vaunia_kolakiala.md)** the mission to **map a fungus-infested region**, an event that set the tone and identity of the group. <br><br>The battle cry “**FUUUUNGUUUUUYS!**” is recorded in several accounts, reflecting the group’s playful yet intense dynamic. | **Origem e Tema / Origin and Theme**<br><br>O conflito central que dá origem ao nome **FunGuys** está ligado à **ameaça recorrente de fungos alienígenas e esporos fúngicos** usados como arma biológica pelo **[Culto do Mal Elemental](../cults/cult_of_elemental_evil.md)**. <br><br>• O grupo nasce da investigação sobre a **corrupção de um riacho**, infestado por um “fungo alienígena”, a pedido do treant **[Borogrove](wild/npc_borogrove.md)**.<br>• A **propagação dos esporos fúngicos** fazia parte de um plano maior do [Culto do Mal Elemental](../cults/cult_of_elemental_evil.md) para **desestabilizar comunidades fluviais**.<br>• O fungo demonstrava uma **capacidade telepática latente**, sugerindo um tipo de **mente colmeia controlada**.<br><br>O episódio estabelece o tema do grupo: uma intersecção entre **corrupção elemental e biológica**, onde natureza e magia se confundem.                                                                                                                                                                       | **Origin and Theme**<br><br>The core conflict that gives the **FunGuys** their name is tied to the **recurring threat of alien fungi and fungal spores** used as a biological weapon by the **[Cult of Elemental Evil](../cults/cult_of_elemental_evil.md)**. <br><br>• The group originated while investigating the **corruption of a stream** infested by an “alien fungus,” at the request of the treant **[Borogrove](wild/npc_borogrove.md)**.<br>• The **spread of fungal spores** was part of a broader plan by the [Cult of Elemental Evil](../cults/cult_of_elemental_evil.md) to **destabilize riverside communities**.<br>• The fungus itself exhibited **latent telepathic properties**, hinting at a **controlled hive-mind**.<br><br>This episode defined the group’s theme: the intersection of **elemental and biological corruption**, where nature and magic blur. [Esgotos de Greyhawk](free_city_of_greyhawk/esgotos_de_greyhawk.md), sob a fachada de eliminar concorrência para a **[Assassins Guild](../guilds/assassins_guild.md)**.<br><br>**S22 & S23 – Semanas 22
